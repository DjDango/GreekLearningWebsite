Articles in greek

Intro

In greek we have a LOT of articles and 5 cases: Nominative, accusative, genitive, vocative and dative. We'll begin with nomiative, nominative is a noun that is the subject of a sentence. There are definitive and indefinitive articles in greek with 3 genders: Masculine, feminine & neuter.

Masculine, feminine & neuter nominative articles

Let's begin immediately, the definite article in english is 'the' and indefinite: 'a' or 'an'. The masculine def. nom. article in greek is 'Ο' in lowercase it's: 'ο', the feminine one is: 'Η' in lowercase: 'η' and for neuter: 'Το' in lowercase: 'το'.
